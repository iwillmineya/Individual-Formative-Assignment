What is Mental health

Mental health is a major factor of your everyday life, it effects rational thinking, relationships, work. but when people are having mental health issues it has a massive negative impact on all of their life, mental health issues are extremely important to pick up on as it can lead to drastic measures, some examples of mental health issues are;
anxiety
depression
PTSD

Asking for help is a sign of strength.

Why mental health is important

mental health is hard to deal with, and often leads to people taking drastic measures. It effects aspects of your everyday life. People out their need help, sometimes it can be hard to see in people. If mental Illness goes undetected it can lead to the person making drastic measures. It is very important to have good mental health as it will help you spot other people struggling aswell. There are many causes for mental health issues, some common causes are;
Childhood trauma
Traumatic events
Genetics
brain injury
It is important to seek help if you have any symptoms and/or events happend.

As a concerned family or friend

It is important you understand the signs they are showing so you dont come off as harsh.
Bring it up to them in a calm manner but not too much pressure
Just hang around with them and see if they reach out.
